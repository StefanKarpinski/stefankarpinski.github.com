Bio: I am one of the co-creators of the Julia programming language and a co-founder of Julia Computing, Inc., which provides support, consulting and training for commercial usage of Julia. I've previously worked as a software engineer and data scientist at Akamai, Citrix Online, and Etsy. In addition to running Julia Computing, I have a part-time appointment as a Research Engineer at New York University as part of the Moore-Sloan Data Science Initiative.
